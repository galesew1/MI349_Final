Super Galaxy Jumper

Super Galaxy Jumper is a 2D platform adventure. Travel to different worlds and even space. Play as an astronaut and explore this 2D adventrue. Your goal is to jump to the end of the level, but there are plenty of different secrets for the players to discover. Enemies and obstacles will challenge the players movement and timing. Can you reach the end of every level?

Space Voyager 3D is a recreation of Super Galaxy Jumper. Space Voyager takes the characters and enemies of Super Galaxy Jumper and brings them into a 3D environment.
